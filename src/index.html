A Student Production, lead by Executive Producer, Gerald Olson, THING on the Beach is a short monster movie shot from the point of view of The Monster, an other-worldly deep sea creature beached after a storm. It will be exciting to watch, but challenging to produce. The unique perspective and shooting on a beach location will be difficult to pull off. Done right, we think it'll produce a spectacular picture, filled with gripping action, exquisitely disgusting practical effects, thrills, chills, and spills!
